How do I input and display Simplified Chinese characters?

To use a Simplified Chinese input method provided by Mac OS X, try
#+BEGIN_SRC emacs-lisp
  (set-language-environment 'Chinese-GB)
  (set-keyboard-coding-system 'chinese-iso-8bit)
#+END_SRC
in your .emacs file.

If you use May '05 edition or later, add one more line.
  : (setq default-input-method "MacOSX-IM-CN")

关键是：
  : (setq default-input-method "MacOSX-IM-CN")
